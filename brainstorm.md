# Brainstorming - AeroPredict

## App Idea
AeroPredict is an iOS app that helps users search flights and view predicted delay risk using smart analytics.

## Problem
Many travelers only see flight status updates after delays happen. They do not get prediction-based insights ahead of time.

## Users
- Travelers
- Frequent flyers
- Students
- Business travelers

## Core Features
- Search for flights
- View delay prediction
- Risk level (Low / Medium / High)
- Save flights
- Dashboard insights

## What makes it different
Focuses on prediction instead of just flight tracking.
